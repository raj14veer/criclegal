# Privacy Policy for CricMath

**Effective Date:** April 28, 2026

CricMath ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the CricMath mobile application ("App").

## 1) What CricMath Does

CricMath is a digital calculator and record-keeping utility for cricket-related calculations and logs.  
It is **not** a gambling platform, does not process real-money betting transactions, and does not provide wallet services.

## 2) Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we and our third-party service providers may collect:

- **Account Information**
  - Email address (for sign up, sign in, password reset via Firebase Authentication)
  - User/account ID generated by Firebase

- **App Data You Enter**
  - Match names, player names, entries, and calculation-related records
  - Stored locally on device and/or in backend services used for app functionality

- **Advertising and Device Data**
  - Device identifiers (such as advertising identifiers, where available)
  - Advertising interaction data (for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention)

- **Diagnostics and Performance Data**
  - Crash logs, app performance metrics, and technical diagnostics

## 3) How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- provide authentication and account access
- operate core app functionality (records/calculations/history)
- maintain app security and prevent abuse
- diagnose crashes and improve performance
- show and measure ads via third-party advertising services

## 4) Tracking and Advertising

CricMath uses third-party ad services (such as **Google AdMob**) that may process device-related data for advertising and measurement.

On iOS, the App may request permission under Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework (`NSUserTrackingUsageDescription`).  
If permission is denied, ad personalization may be limited and non-personalized ads may be shown.

## 5) Third-Party Services

CricMath uses third-party providers, including:

- **Google Firebase** (authentication and related backend services)
- **Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads** (advertising)

These providers process data under their own privacy policies:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Firebase Data and Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- AdMob Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

## 6) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data for money.  
We may share data with service providers (such as Firebase and AdMob) strictly to operate, secure, analyze, and monetize the App through ads.

## 7) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described above, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

## 8) Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data.

You can:
- request account/data deletion by contacting us
- manage tracking consent through iOS settings
- limit ad personalization through device settings (where available)

## 9) Children’s Privacy

CricMath is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction).  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate consent.

## 10) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards and rely on reputable cloud/service providers.  
No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 11) International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on the infrastructure of our service providers.

## 12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
We will post updates on this page and revise the effective date.
