Privacy Policy - CricMath

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CricMath handles information.

1. No Accounts

CricMath does not offer sign-up or login. We do not collect or store email addresses, passwords, or user accounts for this app.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Match names, entries, calculations, and history you create are saved only on your device (for example in browser or app local storage) so the calculator and ledger features work. This data is not uploaded to our servers by the app.

3. Advertising (Third Parties)

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob and its partners may process device and usage-related data according to Google's policies. See Google's privacy policy for details.

4. How We Use Information

  • To run the app and keep your on-device ledger working.
  • To show and measure ads through AdMob.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data for money. We do not operate a cloud database for your match data in this version of the app. Limited data may be processed by ad providers as described above.

6. Deleting Your Data

Because there is no account, you can remove your saved matches and history by deleting them inside the app, clearing the app’s storage, or uninstalling the app. For questions, contact chowdhary1191@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
