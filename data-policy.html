What data this covers

Match and history data you enter in the app (team names, entries, calculations, history) is stored only on your device in the app’s local storage. We do not host a user account or a cloud copy of your ledger for this app.

How to delete your data (no sign-in required)

In the app: Delete individual matches or history entries using the app’s own delete/history actions, or start fresh by clearing what the app stores for your sessions.
On your phone: Android: Settings → Apps → CricMath → Storage → Clear storage (or Clear data). This removes locally saved app data, including your match/history data.
Uninstall the app: Removing the app from your device deletes the app’s local data on that device.

Data we do not control
Advertising: The app uses Google AdMob. Google may process device- and ads-related data under Google’s Privacy Policy. To manage ad-related settings, use your Google and device settings as described in Google’s help. We do not receive a copy of your match ledger on our servers.

Contact
If you have questions about this process, email: chowdhary1191@gmail.com (update if your support email changes).

Retention
On-device match/history data remains until you delete it in the app, clear app data, or uninstall. We do not set a separate 90-day auto-delete for your ledger on your device.
